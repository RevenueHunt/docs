# Get real results from your product quiz

A product quiz is never the goal. It's the means to one: a bigger email list, cheaper ads, a higher average order value, fewer shoppers lost in your catalog. This hub is organized around **what you're trying to achieve for your business**, not around app features.

Everything here is built on what actually works. RevenueHunt has measured **45M+ quiz responses** and **$370M+ in tracked revenue** across **20,000+ brands**. Shoppers who finish a quiz order at roughly **2.75x the rate of a typical store** (more in some categories), spend **11-15% more per order**, and **1 in 5** of their orders land 30+ days later, so a single quiz keeps paying for months. The full breakdown is in the [State of Product Recommendation Quizzes report](https://revenuehunt.com/state-of-product-recommendation-quizzes/){target=_blank}.

    **Pick where you are**, then **pick what you want the quiz to do for your business.** Most great quizzes do several of these at once, so start with your #1 goal and come back for the rest. Every path stays open: read any section, in any order.

## Grow your list and cut your ad costs

If you spend on ads but your list is small and cold, this is your path. A quiz is the best zero-party-data collector you have: shoppers *tell* you their skin type, their goals, their budget, and you can act on it.

- **[Collect the data that's actually worth money](/customer-success/what-data-to-collect/):** ask only the questions whose answers change a recommendation, a segment, or a message.
- **[Turn answers into segments with customer tags](/customer-success/use-customer-tags-in-quiz/):** so a preference like "dry skin" becomes a segment you can act on automatically.
- **[Lower your ad costs with quiz data](/customer-success/use-quiz-data-for-ads/):** build lookalikes from your best quiz takers, retarget by answer, and feed lead-gen ads into the quiz.
- **[Capture more emails without killing completion](/customer-success/ask-marketing-consent/):** when to ask, how to ask, and getting consent right (GDPR/CCPA) without scaring people off.
- **[Send leads straight to your email/SMS platform](/how-to-guides/send-leads-to-klaviyo/):** Klaviyo, and [any other CRM](/how-to-guides/send-leads-to-crm/), with answers attached so flows can segment automatically.
- **[Turn quiz leads into customers](/customer-success/convert-leads-to-customers/):** the follow-up that catches shoppers who don't buy the same day, and 1 in 5 quiz orders land 30+ days later.

## Help shoppers find the right product

When shoppers can't choose, they leave. A quiz turns a confusing catalog into a guided consultation that routes every visitor to the product that fits.

- **[Ask the right questions, and the right number of them](/customer-success/how-to-build-succesful-quiz/):** the best quizzes ask **5-8 questions**; more than that and completion collapses.
- **[Recommend the right products](/how-to-guides/recommend-products/):** set up the logic that maps answers to products so shoppers never get a bad match.
- **[Route a big catalog with a funnel quiz](/how-to-guides/set-up-funnel-quiz/):** split shoppers into sub-catalogs (e.g. skincare / makeup / supplements) from a single quiz.
- **[Get more people to take the quiz](/customer-success/how-to-get-more-quiz-engagement/):** visibility and placement, the highest-ROI fix most stores skip.
- **[Sell across languages and markets](/customer-success/adapt-quizzes-to-markets/):** make every shopper feel the quiz was built for them.

## Learn who your customers are

Personality and "type" quizzes ("What's your skin type?", "Find your scent profile") are engagement magnets: they get shared, and every answer is a declared preference you can use.

- **[Turn answers into segments with customer tags](/customer-success/use-customer-tags-in-quiz/):** the "type" result becomes a segment that powers personalized email and on-site experiences.
- **[Plan a personality / "type" quiz](/customer-success/personality-quizzes/):** define your outcomes, write questions that tell them apart, and decide what each result maps to.
- **[A complete guide to personality-type quizzes](https://revenuehunt.com/guide-to-building-a-personality-type-quiz-with-revenuehunt/){target=_blank}:** what to ask and how to design the result.
- **Feed insights back into your business:** the patterns in your answers tell you which products to stock, bundle, or build next.
